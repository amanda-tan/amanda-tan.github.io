Amanda Tan

Research Computing Technical Lead

Amanda is a self-proclaimed evangelist for data science literacy in Earth Sciences. She graduated from the University of Washington with a PhD in Civil and Environmental Engineering where she worked on understanding the streamflow regimes in the Pan-Arctic, Bhutan, Brazil, Nicaragua and Central Asia. She is now working on understanding the research computing regimes of researchers at the University of Washington.
